## Gymbud
Gymbud is a fitness tracking app that helps users monitor their workouts, set goals, and stay motivated. With a user-friendly interface and a variety of features, Gymbud makes it easy for fitness enthusiasts to track their progress and achieve their fitness goals.
### Features
- Workout tracking: Log your workouts, including exercises, sets, reps, and weights.
- Goal setting: Set fitness goals and track your progress towards achieving them.
- Progress monitoring: View your workout history and track your improvements over time.
- Social sharing: Share your achievements and progress with friends and the Gymbud community.
- Personalized recommendations: Get workout suggestions based on your goals and preferences.
